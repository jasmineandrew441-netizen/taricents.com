About TARICENTS

Your trusted perfume destination in Bayelsa State

Welcome to TARICENTS

TARICENTS is Yenagoa's premier destination for authentic, luxury perfumes. Located in the heart of Biogbolo, we've been serving the fragrance needs of Bayelsa State residents with passion and dedication.

Our mission is to make premium fragrances accessible to everyone in Yenagoa and beyond. We carefully curate our collection to ensure that every perfume we offer meets the highest standards of quality and authenticity.

Whether you're looking for a signature scent, a gift for a loved one, or simply want to explore the world of fine fragrances, TARICENTS is here to guide you on your olfactory journey.
